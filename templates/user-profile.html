CoE Smart Agriculture

About

A Center of Excellence for Agriculture is an institution or entity dedicated to advancing, developing, and enhancing the agricultural sector in a specific region or country. It serves as a focal point for innovation, research, development, and training in the field of agriculture. The primary goal is to improve the quality, productivity, and sustainability of the agricultural sector while advancing knowledge and technology related to agriculture. These centers typically collaborate with various stakeholders, including government agencies, universities, research institutions, farmers, and the private sector.
